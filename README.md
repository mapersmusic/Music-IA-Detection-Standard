# 🎼 Music-AI-Detection-Standard

This repository provides a collaborative framework for identifying AI-generated music through **behavioral analysis** and **metadata auditing**. 

As AI music generation (such as Suno, Udio, and others) becomes more prevalent, distinguishing between human creativity and synthetic content is crucial for transparency, fair royalty distribution, and listener trust.

## 🔍 Our Approach

Unlike automated audio analysis tools that focus on waveforms and spectrograms (which are prone to false positives and rapid obsolescence), this standard relies on **"common sense" criteria** and **verifiable facts** to evaluate artist authenticity.

We focus on the "intention" and the digital footprint behind the music rather than just the sound itself.

## 📊 Key Analysis Indicators

The standard is based on several weighted criteria:

* **Release Velocity**: Detection of abnormal publication rhythms, such as sudden spikes to over 50 tracks per year post-2022.
* **Credit Transparency**: Identification of missing songwriter credits or cases where an artist fails to claim intellectual property rights on a large catalog.
* **Real-World Footprint**: Evaluation of an artist's physical presence, including live performances, interviews, and a verifiable history prior to the AI boom (pre-2020).
* **Technical & Visual Consistency**: Analysis of abnormal loudness patterns (specifically on YouTube Music) and AI-generated artwork artifacts.

## 🟢🟡🔴 The Scoring System

Based on the criteria above, artists are categorized into three states:

1.  **Human Artist**: No suspicious signals detected or profile verified through human moderation.
2.  **Unverified**: Ambiguous signals or analysis currently in progress following a report.
3.  **AI Probable**: High suspicion score validated by manual review.

## 🤝 How to Contribute

This is a **living document**. We believe that the best way to keep up with AI evolution is through community intelligence.

* **Propose Criteria**: Open an Issue or Pull Request to suggest new behavioral red flags.
* **Refine Thresholds**: Help us calibrate the "release velocity" limits based on real-world data.
* **Case Studies**: Share anonymized examples of AI content farms to help improve the detection grid.
